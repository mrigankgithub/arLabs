aRhealth App

Our ultimate tool with unparalleled technology guides you through the complete range of communicable and non-communicable health risks. aRti

aRti-ficial intelligence-based complete healthcare assistant is your personal guide for your health status. It is always ready to assess your health whenever you need it. Just experienced any abnormality and want to know the health risk? Ask aRti and she will fetch you the all necessary and authentic pieces of information regarding the potential diseases and prescribed medications assisting you in the maintenance or restoration of the normal state.

aRisk Assessment

Currently, the most advanced feature to monitor the spread of infectious diseases in the community. Through its high accuracy global risk maps and local risk radars, the feature allows real-time assessment of an individual’s risks of acquiring any infectious disease at different locations via advanced contact tracing technology.

aRnet

First of its kind, the aRnet is the healthcare networking platform designed to update individuals about existing and possibly upcoming health challenges in a community so that the appropriate measures are taken to counter them in time.

aRbase

The database of various disease outbreaks is ready to provide you the complete information to design and manage your healthcare policies.
